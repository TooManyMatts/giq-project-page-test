About the Project

The Geometric IQ Test (GIQ) aims to benchmark monocular 3D reconstruction models on their ability to understand geometric structures of polyhedral objects.

Dataset

We provide synthetic and real-world images of polyhedra inspired by Magnus Wenninger's "Polyhedron Models".
